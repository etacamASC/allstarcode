Telling My Story

Being Guatemalan-American, I'm proud to represent the culture of my ancestors and of the American society. When I look at the world around me, I see much diversity. The United States is truly a nation built with a foundation of immigrants. Unfortunately, there are many people who neglect the importance of immigrants in the nation and use racist terms. Growing up, I've become accustomed to being called a Mexican and having stereotypes surround me. Absolutely no one should have to suffer through the act of racism and this has to stop immediately. I myself do not let the negativity of the world belittle me, instead I let the negativity empower me to do better for myself and for others through technology. One day I hope to be able to help end the act of racism and solve many difficult real-world problems, strongly believing that technology has a big leap towards ultimate greatness for a better society.

Daring Greatly

Absolutely nothing in life worth-having comes easy. To achieve success in life, one has to take many risks. Taking risks are the building stones of success. No one should be afraid to dare greatly. Anything in the world is possible, as long as one believes and takes risk. Personally, I want to create the next-big-thing in the tech industry. Such a dream is surely daring greatly, but I should have nothing to fear except for fear itself and take advantage of the many opportunites provided around me. All Star Code surely has provided me the mentallity strength to dare greatly and not be afraid to reach out to my network to ask for assistance.

Embracing Failure

Failure does not reek of disastor, it flourishes success. Absolutely no one in life has become successful without overcoming failure. Failure should be embraced. Failing is merely not an obstacle, but a partner wanting you to achieve. If one allows failure to end their pursuit for success, then one will not achieve greatness in life and will waste time and money on what could have been valuable work.
